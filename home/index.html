Quick answers

Home physio, made clear.

Do you offer home visits?

Yes. Dr Rutvi provides doctor-led physiotherapy at home across Bhayander to Andheri, including Mira Road, Dahisar, Borivali, Kandivali, Malad, Goregaon and Jogeshwari, by appointment.

What can she help with?

Back and neck pain, sports injuries, post-surgery rehab, knee and joint pain, sciatica, posture, frozen shoulder and elderly mobility.

How do I book?

Tap WhatsApp and share your concern, suburb and preferred day or time. You can also call +91 88794 75065.

How are commercials shared?

Commercials are shared privately after Dr Rutvi understands your concern, suburb, visit requirement and recovery plan.

Which languages work?

English, Hindi, Gujarati and Marathi are supported for patient conversations and booking help.
