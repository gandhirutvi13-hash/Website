Continuity of care
Care philosophy
"Good physiotherapy should make you feel clearer, not dependent."

The goal is not to overwhelm you with exercises or keep you unsure about what is happening. Dr Rutvi explains the problem, guides treatment, and helps you understand what to do between visits so recovery feels manageable.

How she works

The care standards patients can expect.

Continuity

The person who assesses you continues to guide your plan, progress, and follow-up.

Context

Your stairs, chair, bed, desk, and daily routine become part of the assessment.

Clarity

You understand what is being treated, why it matters, and what to practise at home.

Honesty

You get realistic guidance about what physiotherapy can help, what needs time, and when medical review may be needed.

Experience & focus

Musculoskeletal Care
For Real Life.

Musculoskeletal problems often show up during ordinary tasks: bending, climbing stairs, sitting at a desk, walking outside, lifting a child, or returning to sport. Home visits let those real movement demands shape the plan.

Dr Rutvi supports pain, stiffness, injury recovery, post-operative rehabilitation, sports return, senior mobility, posture concerns, and exercise guidance with hands-on care and clear home programming.

Physiotherapy home visit services

Home-visit care by concern.

Musculoskeletal physiotherapy

Neck, back, shoulder, elbow, wrist and hand, hip, knee, ankle and foot pain; muscle strains and ligament sprains; tendinitis and bursitis; joint stiffness; frozen shoulder; tennis elbow and golfer's elbow; plantar fasciitis, heel pain, sciatica, cervical and lumbar spondylosis.

Sports physiotherapy

Sports injury assessment, return-to-sport rehabilitation, injury prevention programs, running injury rehabilitation, cricket, football, badminton, gym and fitness injury rehab, performance enhancement exercises and sports taping.

Post-operative rehabilitation

Total Knee Replacement (TKR), Total Hip Replacement (THR), ACL reconstruction, meniscus surgery, rotator cuff repair, shoulder stabilization surgery, fracture rehabilitation and spine surgery rehabilitation.

Manual therapy

Joint mobilization, soft tissue mobilization, Myofascial Release (MFR), Muscle Energy Techniques (MET), stretching and flexibility therapy.

Pain management

Kinesiology taping, IASTM, cupping therapy, trigger point release, and electrotherapy such as TENS, IFT and ultrasound when suitable portable equipment is available for the home visit.

Exercise therapy

Personalized strengthening, core strengthening, balance and proprioception training, mobility and flexibility exercises, functional rehabilitation, gait training, fall prevention and home exercise prescription.

Geriatric physiotherapy

Arthritis management, osteoporosis exercise programs, balance training, walking training, fall prevention and age-related joint pain management.

Women's health physiotherapy

Pregnancy exercise guidance, core and diaphragm activation, postnatal rehabilitation and posture correction.

Ergonomics & lifestyle

Workstation assessment, posture correction, ergonomic advice for office workers, patient education and activity modification.

Nutrition guidance

Sports nutrition guidance, weight management counselling, lifestyle modification and clinical nutrition advice within scope as a certified nutritionist.
