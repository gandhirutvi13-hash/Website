Dr Rutvi Gandhi

PT, MPT (Musculoskeletal & Sports)

Dr Rutvi founded PhysioByRutvi to raise the standard of home-visit physiotherapy in Mumbai. Frustrated by aggregator platforms that prioritise volume over clinical continuity, she built a practice where every patient receives a proper assessment and an evidence-based plan.

She holds a Master of Physiotherapy specialising in Musculoskeletal and Sports rehabilitation, bringing advanced diagnostic and manual therapy skills directly to the patient's home.
