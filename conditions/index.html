CONDITIONS HUB

Who we help.

Our team provides home-based assessment and rehabilitation for musculoskeletal, orthopaedic and age-related mobility conditions.

Back & Neck Pain

Disc issues, sciatica, postural strain, cervical spondylosis and mechanical back pain.

Sports Injuries

Ligament sprains, muscle strains, tendonitis, runners knee and return-to-sport rehab.

Post-Surgery Rehab

Knee/hip replacements, ACL reconstruction, fracture repairs and spinal surgery recovery.

Knee & Joint Pain

Osteoarthritis, patellofemoral pain, general joint stiffness and age-related wear.

Sciatica & Nerve Pain

Radiating leg or arm pain, numbness, tingling and nerve root impingement.

Frozen Shoulder

Adhesive capsulitis, rotator cuff issues, impingement and restricted overhead movement.

Geriatric Mobility

Fall prevention, balance training, muscle weakness and maintaining independence at home.

Posture & Ergonomics

Desk-related stiffness, tension headaches, upper cross syndrome and ergonomic adjustments.

Strength & Conditioning

Supervised home exercise, post-rehab strengthening and returning to gym or sports safely.
