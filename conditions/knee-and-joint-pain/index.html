Knee and Joint Pain Physiotherapy at Home in Mumbai
KNEE AND JOINT PAIN

Moving better, even with joint wear.

Joint pain often leads to moving less, which ironically makes joints stiffer and surrounding muscles weaker. A home assessment focuses on safely rebuilding the strength needed to support painful joints and keep you active.

Discuss Joint Pain Management

This service may be relevant if

  • ✓ Knees ache when climbing stairs or standing up from a chair.
  • ✓ You have been diagnosed with osteoarthritis or joint degeneration.
  • ✓ Joints feel stiff in the morning or after resting.
  • ✓ You want to delay surgery or manage symptoms conservatively.
  • ✓ Pain is making you avoid walking or daily activities.

What the physiotherapist may assess

  • → Joint mobility, swelling and areas of tenderness.
  • → The strength of muscles that control and absorb load for the joint.
  • → How you walk, sit, stand and manage stairs.
  • → Balance, confidence and fear of movement.
  • → Activity levels and footwear where relevant.
