What the process may include

01

Assessment

A conversation and physical assessment considering symptoms, movement, daily tasks, goals and relevant medical information.

02

Plan

The aim is not to force one “perfect” posture. Assessment considers how long positions are held, movement variety, task setup, strength and recovery habits.

03

Review

Progress is reviewed and the plan is adjusted. Results and timelines differ between people, and no outcome is guaranteed.

Safety and suitability

Assessment comes before a treatment plan.

The information on this website is educational and does not replace individual medical assessment. Some symptoms may require medical review or emergency care before physiotherapy. Where clinically suitable, the physiotherapist will explain options, expected limitations and when referral is appropriate.
