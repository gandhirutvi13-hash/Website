POSTURE & WORK-RELATED DISCOMFORT

Relief for the modern workday.

Hours at a desk or looking down at screens often lead to chronic tension in the neck, shoulders and mid-back. Physiotherapy can help release tight muscles, build endurance in postural muscles, and adjust your workspace setup.

Discuss Desk-Related Discomfort

This service may be relevant if

  • ✓ You experience frequent tension headaches or neck stiffness.
  • ✓ Your mid-back or shoulders ache after a few hours of work.
  • ✓ You constantly feel the need to stretch or 'crack' your back.
  • ✓ You are working from home and lack an ergonomic setup.
  • ✓ Poor posture is starting to affect your exercise or daily activities.

What the physiotherapist may assess

  • → Spinal alignment and areas of muscle tension.
  • → Strength and endurance of deep neck and back muscles.
  • → Mobility of the thoracic spine and shoulders.
  • → Your workstation setup (chair height, screen position, keyboard).
  • → Daily movement habits and breaks.
