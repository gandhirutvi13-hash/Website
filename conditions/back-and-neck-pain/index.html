Back and Neck Pain Physiotherapy at Home in Mumbai
BACK AND NECK PAIN

When sitting, sleeping or ordinary movement starts to hurt.

Back and neck symptoms can affect work, rest, lifting, driving and exercise. A home assessment looks beyond the painful area to understand movement, strength, daily habits and warning signs that may need medical review.

Check Home-Visit Availability

This service may be relevant if

  • ✓ Pain or stiffness increases with sitting, standing, bending or lifting.
  • ✓ Neck discomfort is linked with desk work or prolonged screen use.
  • ✓ Symptoms return repeatedly after temporary relief.
  • ✓ You are struggling to resume normal activity after a minor strain.
  • ✓ Your doctor has recommended physiotherapy.

What the physiotherapist may assess

  • → Symptom history and aggravating movements.
  • → Neck, back, hip and shoulder mobility where relevant.
  • → Strength, control and tolerance for daily tasks.
  • → Workstation, sleeping or lifting habits.
  • → Sensation, reflexes or neural signs when indicated.

How home physiotherapy may help

Treatment may include education, movement modification, mobility work, hands-on techniques and progressive strengthening. The aim is not simply to provide temporary relief, but to make everyday movement more manageable and build the capacity required for work, exercise and daily life.

Seek medical advice promptly if

There is significant trauma, fever, unexplained weight loss, new weakness, worsening numbness, loss of bladder or bowel control, severe unrelenting pain or other symptoms that concern you.
