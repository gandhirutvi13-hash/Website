SPORTS INJURY REHABILITATION

Return to activity with a plan—not guesswork.

A sports injury needs more than waiting for pain to settle. Rehabilitation should rebuild the movement, strength, control and confidence required by the activity you want to return to.

Discuss Your Injury

This service may be relevant if

  • ✓ You have a recent sprain, strain or overuse injury.
  • ✓ Pain returns whenever training intensity increases.
  • ✓ You can manage daily activity but do not feel ready for sport.
  • ✓ You are returning after a period of rest, immobilisation or surgery.
  • ✓ You want a structured strengthening and return-to-activity plan.

What the physiotherapist may assess

  • → Injury history, swelling, tenderness and movement tolerance.
  • → Strength and control compared with the uninjured side.
  • → Running, landing, jumping, lifting or sport-specific movement where appropriate.
  • → Training load, recovery pattern and previous injuries.
  • → Signs that require imaging or an orthopaedic opinion.
