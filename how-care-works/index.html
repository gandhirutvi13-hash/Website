HOW CARE WORKS

Clearer matching, assessment and follow-through.

We believe home physiotherapy should not feel like an unpredictable dispatch service. By centralising our intake through Dr Rutvi, we aim to pair you with the most appropriate physiotherapist for your condition and location.

1

Initial Contact

You send a WhatsApp message detailing your symptoms, location, and preferred timing. Dr Rutvi reviews this personally. If home physiotherapy is not appropriate for your symptoms, we will tell you.

2

Team Assignment

Based on availability, area, and condition, Dr Rutvi will either schedule the visit herself or assign a qualified physiotherapist from the team. We confirm the practitioner’s name and time before they arrive.

3

The Assessment (45–60 Mins)

The physiotherapist evaluates your movement, strength, and pain triggers. They explain the likely cause of your symptoms and outline a treatment plan. If hands-on treatment is appropriate, it begins in this session.

4

Ongoing Treatment & Review

Follow-up sessions (typically 45 minutes) combine manual therapy for relief and exercises for strength. Clinical notes are maintained. Dr Rutvi oversees complex cases and is available to review progress if a second opinion is needed internally.
