OUR TEAM

A clinically led practice.

We are not an aggregator platform that randomly assigns contractors. PhysioByRutvi operates as a unified practice led by Dr Rutvi Gandhi. Every physiotherapist who visits your home is qualified, vetted, and follows our standard of care.

Qualifications

  • → All team members hold a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) at minimum.
  • → Many hold or are pursuing a Master of Physiotherapy (MPT).
  • → Registered with the appropriate professional councils.

Continuity of Care

  • → We aim to keep you with the same physiotherapist throughout your recovery.
  • → If scheduling requires a substitution, clinical notes are shared seamlessly.
  • → Complex cases can be reviewed internally by Dr Rutvi for a second opinion.
