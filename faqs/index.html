Common Questions

How long is a session?

Initial assessments take between 45 and 60 minutes. Follow-up treatment sessions are typically 45 minutes, depending on the complexity of the condition.

Do you bring equipment?

Yes. We carry basic assessment tools, resistance bands, kinesiology tape, and portable electrotherapy devices (like TENS/IFT) if required. We also creatively use your home environment (chairs, stairs, beds) for functional rehabilitation.

What are your charges?

Pricing depends on the exact location and the nature of the required care. Please message us on WhatsApp with your suburb and concern for accurate pricing.

Can I request a female physiotherapist?

Yes, our team includes both male and female physiotherapists. You may request a preference when booking your initial assessment.
